About

At Healthy Veggie you will find great food facts and recipes to help you eat your way to good health.

Mind you this is not just for those on a diet! Rather Healthy Veggie is for anyone who wants to eat and feed healthy food.

In our blog section you'll find useful information about super foods, nutrition and health boosters. Our Recipes page is full of mouth watering vegetarian recipes to create healthy and delicious meals. And finally there's Caloriebot a friendly robot, who'll tell you how many calories you need and calculate how many calories you eat in a day!

Thanks so much for visiting Healthy Veggie!
